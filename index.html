Our Blueprint for Success

The Standard Operating Procedure for Sarik & Nibir's Freelancing Venture

Our Business Model

Our strategy is simple yet powerful. We acquire projects through highly optimized gigs on our personal Fiverr accounts. The technical work is then delegated to a vetted network of talented engineering students from BUET, ensuring high-quality delivery while we manage client relations and growth.

Fair Revenue Distribution

We believe in fair compensation. After Fiverr's platform fee, 60% of the net revenue goes directly to the student freelancer who executed the work. The remaining 40% is reinvested into the business and shared between the partners.

How We Work: Project Flow

Step 1: Order Confirmation

A client places an order on Fiverr. Partners are notified.

Step 2: Delegation

Project requirements are passed to Nibir for talent assignment.

Step 3: Project Execution

A vetted BUET freelancer works on the project.

Step 4: Quality Assurance

Both partners conduct a thorough review to ensure perfection.

Step 5: Delivery & Payout

Final work is delivered to the client and the freelancer is paid promptly.

Our Roadmap to Growth

We have a clear, milestone-based plan for scaling our operations from a partnership into a fully-fledged technology company. Our growth is tied to sustainable performance, not just cumulative earnings.

Milestone 1: Company Registration

Trigger: $20,000 Cumulative Profit

We will register as a limited liability company (LLC) to formalize our business structure, streamline banking, and manage taxes professionally.

Milestone 2: Hiring Permanent Staff

Trigger: $10,000+ Monthly Profit

Once we achieve consistent high profitability, we will hire permanent staff for key roles to increase our capacity and service quality.

Milestone 3: Securing Office Space

Trigger: $20,000+ Monthly Profit for 3 Months

With substantial and stable revenue, we will establish a physical office to create a collaborative hub for our growing team.
