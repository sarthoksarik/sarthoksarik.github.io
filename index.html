How We Work: Detailed Project Flow

Step 1: Order Confirmation

A client places an order. The partner who received it immediately notifies the other and clarifies all project requirements with the client.

Step 2: Delegation & Handoff

Nibir communicates the project opportunity to the talent pool, including the full scope, deadline, and payout, then selects the best freelancer.

Step 3: Project Execution

The assigned freelancer works on the project. Nibir acts as the project manager, monitoring progress and providing support.

Step 4: Internal Quality Assurance

The completed work is submitted for a mandatory review by both Sarik and Nibir. An iterative feedback cycle ensures the work is flawless.

Step 5: Delivery & Financial Reconciliation

The finalized project is delivered to the client. Once payment clears, Nibir updates the financial ledger and pays the freelancer within 3 business days.

Our Roadmap to Growth

We have a clear, milestone-based plan for scaling our operations from a partnership into a fully-fledged technology company. Our growth is tied to sustainable performance.

Milestone 1: Company Registration

Trigger: $20,000 Cumulative Profit

We will register as a limited liability company (LLC) to formalize our business structure, streamline banking, and manage taxes professionally.

Milestone 2: Hiring Permanent Staff

Trigger: $10,000+ Monthly Profit

Once we achieve consistent high profitability, we will hire permanent staff for key roles to increase our capacity and service quality.

Milestone 3: Securing Office Space

Trigger: $20,000+ Monthly Profit for 3 Months

With substantial and stable revenue, we will establish a physical office to create a collaborative hub for our growing team.
